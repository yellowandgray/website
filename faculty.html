Our STAFF

R K Public School ensures safe, secure and joyful experience in the process of learning helped by passionate and knowledgeable educators. Only a passionate and committed teacher can make a difference in a child’s world. Such a teacher effortlessly shapes up the student’s life through his/her patience, love and care, not to speak of the appropriate knowledge. Our well-trained teachers would mentor not only the student, but also the parent where required and become role models. We, at R K Public School believe that



ONE book, ONE pen, ONE child,

And

"ONE teacher”

Can change the world.
