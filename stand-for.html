Our VISION

To be a world class educational institution providing holistic education ensuring all round personality development of the students to enable them to thrive as a global citizen in this multicultural, pluralistic society embracing the richness and diversity of our culture.


Our MOTTO

Amrutham Tu Vidya...

(Knowledge is Eternal)

Knowledge is the only weapon which destroys ignorance; It is knowledge which takes one to the highest wisdom; It is also the worthiest asset for any human being. Hence, knowledge is known as the sacred


AMRUTH — " THE DIVINE NECTAR".

In the ancient Indian system of education, Knowledge was imparted through interaction with the learned Teacher, who most probably acquired it through intuition and by being one with Nature. Thus knowledge that passed on to the student was not ordinary; it was the Eternal Knowledge, the one which fulﬁlled all the requirements for a successful life.





It is possible to impart Eternal Knowledge in a present day school by incorporating its four main components namely:

Preparation

Inspiration

Leadership

Realisation

The system of education should PREPARE the student with necessary fundamentals to receive the knowledge.


It must INSPIRE not only the student but also the teacher to effectively involve themselves towards the goals.


The student must be guided, supported and counselled at every stage to become a LEADER.


The system should make the student REALISE his/her true potential by channelizing his/her inner urges towards an integrated personality in due course.


Thus, a child educated in the above manner would be armed with knowledge, both practical and intuitive, which would make his/her life-journey fulﬁlling.


R K PUBLIC SCHOOL, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI, is an ediﬁce built on these four pillars of ancient wisdom.



OUR AIMS AND OBJECTIVES.

  • Provide modern facilities for learning and ensure an environment in which students are challenged to seek excellence academically, socially, physically, artistically and spiritually.

  • Provide an environment where all students and teachers feel welcomed, respected and trusted.

  • Provide a balanced importance to academics, computers, physical education and performing and visual arts and also integrate them with each other in a planned manner.

  • Provide quality education in a constantly changing world by combining the current techniques in curriculum, learning and teaching styles with the traditional educational approaches.

  • Ensure highly effective and committed teachers who are constantly encouraged to develop their own professional skills and provide quality teaching, coaching and mentoring focussed on improving student performance.

  • Ensure that the teachers and students realise that learning is a life time process and inculcate in them the desire to learn.

  • Develop basic life skills among students which form the foundation for a sound education like reading, numeracy, communication and research methods.

  • Develop, improve and nurture thinking skills among students.

  • Enable students to reach their full potential by developing their self— esteem and their innate talents taking into account each one’s individual differences.

  • Inculcate in students a sense of responsibility and self— discipline.

  • Ensure an atmosphere of cooperation and consultation between all members of the school and the parents.

  • Understand, learn and take pride in the rich cultural diversity and history of our ancient land and its value systems.

  • Ensure that "LEARNING IS A JOY” because what you learn with joy remains with you lifelong.
