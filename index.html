About The Foundation

Dr. V. Krishnamurthy Education Foundation manage the century old National College, Tiruchirappalli. The Foundation is extending its scope to primary and secondary eduction through the Rajam Krishnamurthy Public School (CBSE), Tiruchirappalli starting from the academic year 2018-19.

About Our PRESIDENT



Presently Dr.V. Krishnamurthy is also the Chancellor of Central University of TamilNadu (Thiruvarur) and indian Maritime University (Chennai). He is also Member, Board of Governors, Administrative Staff College of India, Hyderabad.

Dr. V. Krishnamurthy has been associated with National College for the last 9 years as its President during which period the college has been transformed into a Centre of excellence securing the highest grade rating of A+ by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).

About RK PUBLIC SCHOOL (CBSE)



The Rajam Krishnamurthy Public School (R K Public School) is born out of the accumulated heritage of a century of educational experience and national spirit embodied in the prestigious institution popularly known as NATIONAL COLLEGE, Tiruchirappalli, which was founded in 1919.

The National college alumnus includes among others Mr. R. Venkataraman, the Eighth President of india and padma Vibhushan, Dr. C. Rangarajan, Former Governor RBI and former Chairman Economic Advisory Council to the Prime Minister.
