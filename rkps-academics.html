OUR CURRICULUM

As the famous quote by Benjamin Franklin goes , ” Tell me and i forget; Teach me and I remember; Involve me and I learn". We, at R K Public school follow the practical way of learning by involving the children through our Learner Oriented Interactive Curriculum (LOIC) which aims at bringing in 'Developmental Appropriateness’. The best Academic institutions are not those that produce simply excellent results, but those that churn out good citizens imbibed with traditional values alongside modern life skills, through balanced training of the whole individual. They encourage the personality to imagine even the impossible and venture out to achieve the same. R K Public School is thus dedicated to take in a child and send out a scholar.


Our CURRENT PROGRAMS

Early Childhood education plays a very important role in terms of a Child’s Mental, Physical, Intellectual and Emotional development. A very high proportion of Learning takes place during ages zero to six. Our programs are carefully crafted, keeping in mind the need of the children and foster their all—round natural development.





PRE-KINDERGARTEN (pre-KG) PROGRAM

This program will be the child's ﬁrst step towards social engagement in small groups. Pre School staff need presence, time, words, print, and intention to share language and literacy with the children. The focus will be on helping the child take initiatives, build vocabulary, largely through a blend of indoor & outdoor activities such as observation through nature walks, listening to stories and words, engagement in simple games, make-believe and block play and many more, driven by their own instincts. At this stage, children have an attraction to rhyme, rhythm & song and use great deal of muscle activity and co-ordination and they seek opportunities to take the initiative on all that they think they can do and will be encouraged in that area.

KINDERGARTEN

Our Kindergarten classrooms are vibrant and full of life. The Kindergartener is guided in preparatory foundation skills of listening & speaking, Group participation, hand & eye co-ordination for cutting, pasting, writing, sharing & turn-taking, group participation & self-regulation. Since this stage is a period of Vocabulary explosion, a lot of reading, print, alphabet, number awareness and conversing will occur. A great deal of structured activities such as waiting on a line, holding hands to stay as pairs, threading beads, engage in sharing activities and the beginnings of group play will take place


AFTER SCHOOL PROGRAM

R K Public School offers various other After School programs such as Children’s yoga, Sanskrit and other suitable programs.
